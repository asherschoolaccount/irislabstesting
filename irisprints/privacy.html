Privacy Policy

How Iris Prints handles your information

Our Practices

Information Collection: Iris Prints collects only the basic information necessary to process orders — such as your name, order specifications, and payment confirmation.

Use of Information: Information is used strictly for fulfilling orders and communicating with customers about their prints.

Sharing of Information: Iris Prints does not share customer information with third parties, except where required by school rules or applicable law.

Parental Awareness: If a parent or guardian provides funds for a print, it is the customer's responsibility to inform them. Iris Prints is not responsible for disputes between customers and parents regarding money.

Data Retention: Order information is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill orders and track payment.

Security: Iris Prints takes reasonable precautions to keep customer information safe and confidential.
